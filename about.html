Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming Kenya's premier tyre and rim specialists, our story is one of dedication, quality, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Founded on Excellence

Established in 2018, BM Tyres & Rims Express began with a simple vision: to provide Kenya with access to premium quality tyres and rims backed by professional service that exceeds expectations.

What started as a small workshop has grown into a trusted name across Kenya, serving thousands of satisfied customers with our commitment to quality, reliability, and 24/7 emergency service.
